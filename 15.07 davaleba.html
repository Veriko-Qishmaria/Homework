Robotic Process Automation (RPA) can provide analysis and data from a patient’s
condition and their progression. It has the ability to monitor the patient’s
journey from the time they started showing the symptoms to their current condition.
It also offers a smart diagnosis and options for the treatment.
This technology analyzes data from a number of healthcare providers greatly improving the
care cycle as it streamlines a portion of their work.
RPA can also be effective in care management, improving the quality of coordinating care,
population wellness, remote monitoring, and case & utilization management.
